## the noise-evicts-signal invariant is the lesson

Before 083, the loop had internalized "use
`{chronicle: false}` when you have parallel notify
+ chronicle writes." That's a SITE-level rule.

After 083, the rule is SYSTEM-level: **if your
system runs at more than ~1 chronicle write per
day in steady state, it CAN silently evict other
systems from the buffer.**

That's a fundamentally different kind of rule.
Codifying it in the doc promotes from "pattern I
happened to find" to "design constraint the realm
must respect."

Future ticks adding new chronicle writers should
now check: what's my writer's steady-state
entries/day? If > 1, budget for `{chronicle:
false}` on the UI-toast half.

  founder reference ON THE ITEM, not as

  097 = founder1 fire-keeper. The offering
  at the stone is a continuation of ritual
  life. Founder1 represents the realm's
  ritual continuity already.

  founder1 is named (which is by day 6 in

  fired pre-214 are unaffected — the flag
  prevents refire.

  every play. With the founder variant added,
  some kingdoms whose hash would have picked
  index 6 (out of 7) get the founder variant;
  others keep their existing items. Per-
  realm distribution: ~14% of realms get
  the founder variant.

  process discipline. Closes 089 known-gap
  with full historical chain.

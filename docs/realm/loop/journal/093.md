## why this shape

- **Uses NARRATIVE_BEATS (090's pattern) as native ground.**
  092 proved the pattern carries cross-file weight; 093 uses
  it for tonal-arc narrative. Each entry is 1-2 table rows —
  adding the 4th/10th/20th winter would be additive.
- **Day thresholds via season-math:** 7-day seasons, 28-day
  years, winter starts day 22 of each year. So year-2-winter
  reachable at day 50, year-3-winter at day 78, year-5-winter
  at day 134. Day thresholds are loose (the realm may enter
  winter at day 50, 51, 52, 53, or 54 depending on timing) —
  the flag-gate prevents re-fire.
- **Skipped year 4.** 088 filed said "2nd, 3rd, and 5th." The
  gap between 3rd and 5th creates a narrative sense of time
  compressing — 2nd and 3rd close together, then 5th feels
  farther. Year 4 missing is a design choice, not a gap.
- **Founder1 only (not 2 or 3).** 088 established founder1 as
  "the one who stands in the fields" — the realm's storied
  founder. Keeping the subsequent-winter beats on founder1
  means one consistent character ages through the 4-beat arc
  (088 + 093×3). Founder2 and founder3 remain unnamed in
  winter; they belong to other surfaces (089 nightmare uses
  all 3, offering may name any, etc.).
- **Graceful degradation to "The realm"/"The settlers"/"The
  oldest in the realm"** varies per-beat — no identical
  fallback prose across three entries. A kingdom whose 072
  didn't fire still gets coherent (but less personal) beats.
- **Tonal arc chosen for meaning, not exposition.**
  - 2nd winter: EXPERIENCE ("knows what to store, what to
    wait out")
  - 3rd winter: OBSERVATION ("counts them like elders")
  - 5th winter: RECEDING MEMORY ("speaks of it less often
    now")
  Each is earned by the implicit passage of game-time —
  nothing in game state directly represents Lira aging;
  the prose carries the arc alone.
- **Tag: milestone, auto-echo-eligible via
  `_ECHO_SOURCE_TAGS`.** A 059 echo decades-later could
  resurface "Lira counted the third winter like elders" as
  "Old folk remember Lira counting winters like elders." The
  founder's quiet weight compounds over play.

## what market.svg expresses that canvas struggles with

- **Scalloped awning fringe.** Canvas market
  has a flat bottom edge on the awning;
  SVG ships the authentic medieval-market
  awning silhouette with 4 scallop drops
  along the bottom. Reads instantly as
  "fabric awning" rather than "rect."
- **Support posts.** Canvas market awning
  visually floats above the counter; SVG
  adds 2 wooden posts at either end making
  the structural form readable.
- **Bunting beneath awning.** 4 small
  triangular pennants on a string. Canvas
  has no bunting; SVG adds it implying
  "marketplace day" or "festival."
- **Bread with score lines.** Canvas bread
  is `fillRect(s.x-6, s.y-12, 4, 3)` —
  plain gold rectangle. SVG ships a rounded
  loaf with diagonal score lines (baker's
  authentic top marks).
- **Apples with stem + leaf + highlight.**
  Canvas apples are plain green rects. SVG
  ships radial-gradient circles + stems +
  leaves + spec highlights.
- **Coin pile / tip jar.** Canvas has just
  bread + apples; SVG adds a small coin
  pile in between for the "marketplace
  exchange" reading.
- **Stacked crate.** Canvas has one crate;
  SVG adds a smaller stacked crate on top
  for the "trade is good — extra inventory"
  reading.
- **Cobblestones underfoot.** Market square
  texture. Canvas market has plain ground
  beneath; SVG adds 7 cobblestone ovals
  (matches the 175 tavern path treatment).

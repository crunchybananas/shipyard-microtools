## why this shape

- **YEAR-1 SPECIFIC fire window.** Summer is
  days 8-14 in year 1 (28-day year, 7-day
  seasons). Year-2 summer is days 36-42 — the
  `day <= 14` clause excludes year 2+. **Fires
  only in year 1.** Most players who play a
  realm past day 7 will see this.
- **Anti-anxious mood.** The realm's narrative
  register is mostly settling-striving (first
  builds), naming-claiming (founders), or
  noticing (snow / nightmare / stone). Almost
  none of the realm's prose says "things are
  okay right now." This beat does. Reads as
  permission to be at rest.
- **No founder reference.** Per 157 post-
  moratorium soft-cap. The "no one needs
  anything" subject is collective.
- **Non-forgetting.** Per 195 saturation flag.
  This is an OPENING beat (acknowledging a
  moment) rather than a closure (acknowledging
  fading).
- **Tag: misc.** Joins the elder-saying /
  observational cluster (148 wanderer / 152
  night-shape / 166 frog-voices / 174 first-
  frost / 184 stone-forgotten / 190
  constellation-forgotten / 193 landmark / now
  196 first-long-evening). 9 misc-tag beats
  total — substantial register weight.
- **Static prose, no kingdom-hash variants.**
  The "first long evening" is universal to all
  realms. No reason for variation.
- **Two-sentence shape.** First sentence
  states the moment. Second sentence
  particularizes (heat / fire). Matches the
  existing observational-beat rhythm.

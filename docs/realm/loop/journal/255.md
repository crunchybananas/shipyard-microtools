## animation: 95%-rest punctuation cycle

Cupola bell mirrors church's 236 punctuation
animation but with rest band stretched to
**95%** (vs church's 85%). 72s cycle (vs
church 32s). 4 swings of decreasing amplitude
(-7°/+5°/-4°/+2°) over ~3.6s burst.

Reads as: townhall bell rings for rare
civic moments — rarer than the church's
regular service. Hierarchically RARER than
the existing rare-est animation in the
sprite roster.

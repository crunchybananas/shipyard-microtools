### LOW (1) — pre-311 saves: silent_morning gate fails after load

**Discovered by code-audit, not [play].**

311 changed silent_morning gate from
`G.buildings.some(b => b.type ===
'church')` to `G.stats?.everHadBuilding?.
church`.

For PRE-311 saves: at save-time the
`everHadBuilding` field did not exist;
loadGame at save.js:207 spreads `s.stats`
over G.stats but cannot resurrect a
field that was never saved. Result:
G.stats.everHadBuilding remains the
empty `{}` from initial state, even
when G.buildings has church.

Net: a player who continues a pre-311
save with church present will NEVER
fire silent_morning_known. They'd need
to PLACE A NEW church post-311 for the
flag to set.

**Severity LOW** — edge case for old-
save players; missed beat is one of 65;
silent_morning was specifically chosen
at 311 because its prose still works
post-substrate-loss.

**Mitigation (~3 LoC)**: in loadGame,
after stats restore, populate
everHadBuilding from currently-loaded
G.buildings:

```js
G.stats.everHadBuilding = G.stats.everHadBuilding || {};
for (const b of G.buildings || []) {
  G.stats.everHadBuilding[b.type] = true;
}
```

This is a backfill. Filed as 316 [code]
for next fixer slot.

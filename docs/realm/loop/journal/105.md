# 105 — the-fixer (smith combat-bonus mechanic)

**Challenge:** the-fixer — accepted. Third in the 101/102/105
character-mechanic arc. Named smith adds +5% projectile damage
to tower/barracks fire. Same pattern, smaller scope each time
as the shape generalizes.

## the change

`docs/realm/js/combat.js:updateTowers` — 2 lines added:

```js
const smithMult = G.namedCharacters?.smith ? 1.05 : 1;
G.projectiles.push({
  ..., damage: 10 * smithMult, ...
});
```

Applied at the projectile-creation site inside the tower+
barracks fire loop. Both building types benefit equally.

## why this shape

- **Matches 101/102 exactly.** Ternary `? 1.N : 1`, applied to
  existing multiplier chain. Three ticks in, the pattern is
  boilerplate.
- **+5% same as merchant.** Uniform multiplier across combat/
  trade/research feels right — the "big 3" character mechanics
  are now on the same scale.
- **Projectile damage, not fire rate.** Two axes could reward
  smith: faster fire OR bigger hit. Damage is simpler
  (multiplicative on a single number); fire rate would
  require modifying `fireTimer = 60` math. Damage also
  stacks better with future upgrades (siege, enchanted
  arrows) if any ever land.
- **Both tower AND barracks benefit.** Both use the same
  projectile creation path. Unified mechanic.

## what I did NOT do

- **Ship the bard/mayor/rival bonuses in this tick.** One
  mechanic per tick keeps the verification scope clean. 101
  journal noted "pattern clean, could ship remaining in 3-
  tick windows each." 105 is the third such window.
- **Add fire-rate bonus as a second axis.** Tempting (smith
  improves BOTH damage AND cadence of fire) but then the
  smith bonus would dominate vs 101/102's single-axis
  approach. Keeping parity.
- **Apply bonus to wall damage** (line 19: `wall.hp -= 0.5 *
  G.speed`) or citizen combat (line 76). Those are ENEMY
  attack paths, not player-side. Smith is the realm's smith;
  bonuses shouldn't help attackers.
- **Cumulative-balance check.** After all 6 characters land,
  that's the right time. Filed (101 idea).

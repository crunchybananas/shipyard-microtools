## why this surprises

This introduces **NEGATIVE-SPACE WEATHER**
to the corpus. The 3 existing weather-
recognition beats are all about weather
ARRIVING:
- 147 great-storm-remembered: storm hit
- 174 first-frost-marked: frost arrived
- 246 first-thaw-known: thaw arrived

272 is about weather **NOT ARRIVING**:
the storm builds offshore, lightning a
long way off, the rain falls on the next
ridge. The realm receives the night
intact as a GIFT it did not earn.

The lift line "is given the night intact"
uses passive voice — acknowledging GRACE.
The realm doesn't WIN safety from the
storm; it RECEIVES safety. Different
philosophical posture from the existing
weather-arrival beats (which are
declarative/observational, not
gift-receiving).

No other beat in the 50-entry corpus
captures the WHAT-DIDN'T-HAPPEN axis.
This is the surprise.

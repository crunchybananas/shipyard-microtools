## why this shape

- **Warm-wood family palette per 181 review.**
  Wood `[0.62, 0.42, 0.20]` matches house +
  bakery in 3D + the SVG sprite tone. Amber
  roof `[0.66, 0.45, 0.16]` matches pitched-
  roof family.
- **Roof-top flag preserved.** 051 added the
  flag in canvas drawTavern as a silhouette-
  distinguishability fix; SVG ships it; 3D
  ships it. All three layers carry the same
  silhouette-affordance.
- **Side sign board with bracket arm.** Two
  boxes: the gold board on the left face +
  a small horizontal arm connecting it to
  the wall. Reads as "wall-mounted sign"
  rather than "free-floating gold cube."
- **Reused pushBox + pushPyramid only.** No
  new mesh primitive needed. Tavern is
  rectangular enough that boxes + a single
  pyramid express the form.
- **Static flag.** Matches 167 windmill +
  183 barracks: 3D animation deferred for a
  future shader-side tick.
- **Door as small dark box.** Same pattern as
  granary 163, barracks 183. "Front face of
  building has a darker rectangle" reads as
  door at iso scale.

## why this shape

- **Diversifies away from misc tag** per 213
  finding 1. tag:character was last used at
  138 (shepherds_song) and is echo-eligible
  per 060/063. The bard joins teacher/
  merchant/smith/rival as named characters
  with literary surfaces beyond their 034
  silent introduction.
- **Quiet interiority shape** — the bard is
  alone, walking, half-forgetting their own
  composition. NOT a mechanical effect (those
  are silent per 034). Just personhood.
- **Avoids META-frame** per 213 finding 2.
  "Some songs the realm has had were never
  sung aloud" is a TRUTH about the realm's
  fabric, not a comment on whether the
  chronicle witnesses what the realm doesn't.
- **Closes with a generalizing line.** Same
  craft as 184 stone-forgotten ("the youngest
  in the realm have never heard the story
  of who found it") and 199 first-cold-morning
  ("the realm pulls a cloak closer and goes
  on") — beat ends with a line that lifts
  beyond the specific moment.
- **kingdom-name-INDEPENDENT but bard-name-
  DEPENDENT.** Uses the kingdom-hashed bard
  name (set at ensureBard); each realm sees
  a different name but the same prose shape.
- **Earned-state gate.** day ≥ 25 ensures
  bard has existed for ~19+ days post-church-
  build (typical church day 5-10 → bard day
  5-10 → +19 = day 24-29). spring/summer
  matches "walking back from the river"
  weather.

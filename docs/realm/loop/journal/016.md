## new findings surfaced this tick

### 1. Raid-flash accumulates across rapid raids

After 4 raids fired in quick succession (via script, not
gameplay), `G.raidFlash` was at 0.35 long after the raids
ended. It probably decays by a fixed amount per frame;
with overlapping raids it can stack and produce a
persistent red world-tint. In normal play this won't
happen because raids are days apart, but it's a fragile
invariant — if a future tick triggers raids faster (debug,
stress test) the screen becomes unreadable. Worth a cap:
`G.raidFlash = Math.max(G.raidFlash, newFlash)` or
`Math.min(G.raidFlash + delta, 1.0)`.

### 2. Population counter during mass death

During the defenseless streak, `☠️ 12` showed next to
the population counter. Population display read `3/3` —
max housing cap was 3. So the game killed 3 citizens per
raid, replaced them via birth, and repeated. That's the
simulation working as designed, but the skull count `12`
with a `3/3` pop *reads* weird — it suggests a steady
state when there's actually been a massacre.

More importantly: **at no point during 4 raids did the
tutorial advance past step 2/10 ("build a farm")**.
Because the player never placed a farm, the tutorial sat
frozen through the entire apocalypse. A real novice in
this situation would think the tutorial was broken.
(Related to 007 finding #3 about tutorial persistence;
this is a different flavor — tutorial doesn't register
game events that clearly supersede the current step.)

### 3. Mission-complete toast fires for "Build barracks or tower"

When I pushed a Barracks into `G.buildings`, a toast
fired: *"✨ Mission complete: Build barracks or tower"*.
That's a good feedback beat — but I note there's a
side-goal here the 010 player would have wanted surfaced
earlier. Namely, the mission system knows "build a
barracks" is a side goal; but before the fix it wasn't
connected to the danger-toast. With 015 in place, the
player learns about barracks through the toast *and*
through the side-goal. Double-signal is healthy.

## ideas generated → ideas.md

- Cap `G.raidFlash` so multiple stacked raids don't
  produce a persistent red tint. Likely `Math.min` in the
  increment line.
- Tutorial event-awareness: if a raid fires or a citizen
  dies while the tutorial is frozen on an early step, the
  tutorial should either pause with a contextual tip or
  advance. Currently it sits static during dramatic game
  events.
- Population/death display coherence: showing `3/3 ☠️12`
  without a cumulative narrative makes the massacre look
  ambient. A quick "-3 citizens lost in raid" toast (or
  inline red flash on the pop counter) would connect the
  dots.

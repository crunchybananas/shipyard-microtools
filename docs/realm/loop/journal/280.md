## why this surprises

This is the **FIRST LIMINAL-MOMENT BEAT**
in the corpus. All previous beats observe:
- The realm doing things (240/245/247/249/
  252/253 character actions, 270 confluence)
- The realm noticing thresholds crossed
  (211/228/230 sustained-state beats)
- The realm noticing absences (272 storm-
  not-arriving, 277 absent-citizen)
- The realm's interior states (254
  nights-blur, 263 chronicle-self)
- POV inversions (275 child-totalizing)

280 introduces a **NEW ANGLE**: noticing
the GAPS between actions. The unactioned
moment — the universal pause between
work-end and rest — recurring every
evening but never NAMED.

The lift line "no one has named this
moment but everyone knows it" captures
collective silent recognition. The realm
shares awareness of moments it has not
given language to. This is a meta-
linguistic observation: the realm
acknowledges things ARE without
acknowledging them VERBALLY.

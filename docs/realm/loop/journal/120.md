## research bonus math

`tech.js:updateResearch`:
```js
const schools = G.buildings.filter(b => b.type === 'school' && b.workers.length > 0).length;
const teacherBonus = G.namedCharacters?.teacher ? 0.1 : 0;
const speedMult = 1 + schools * 0.5 + teacherBonus;
G.currentResearch.progress += G.speed * speedMult;
```

**Tech-time samples from tech.js:**
- masonry: `time: 300`
- engineering: `time: 400`
- metallurgy: `time: 500`
- commerce: `time: 350`
- military: `time: 450`
- brewing: `time: 250`
- architecture: `time: 800`
- baking: `time: 350`
- husbandry: `time: 300`
- smithing: `time: 400`
- archery: `time: 400`

11 researchable techs, total time ≈ **4500 tick-units**.

**Scenarios (no-teacher, baseline):**
- 0 schools (no research): progress += G.speed × 1.0
- 1 school active: progress += G.speed × 1.5 (50% faster)
- 2 schools active: progress += G.speed × 2.0
- 3 schools: × 2.5

**With teacher (post-101):**
- 0 schools: × 1.1 (+10%)
- 1 school: × 1.6 (vs 1.5 = +6.7% relative)
- 2 schools: × 2.1 (vs 2.0 = +5% relative)
- 3 schools: × 2.6 (vs 2.5 = +4% relative)

**Key finding:** teacher bonus is +10% absolute, but its
RELATIVE impact DIMINISHES as more schools come online.
At 3 schools it's +4%. A dedicated-research realm sees
less proportional benefit than a 1-school realm.

**Savings on full tech tree (4500 tick-units):**
- 2 schools no teacher: 4500 / 2.0 = 2250 sim-ticks
- 2 schools + teacher: 4500 / 2.1 = 2143 sim-ticks
- **Saved: 107 sim-ticks** = ~47 seconds at G.speed=4

For a typical 200-day playthrough (~5 real minutes at
speed 4×), teacher saves ~47 seconds of research wait.
**Noticeable but not game-breaking.**

## trade bonus math

`trade.js:executeTrade`:

```js
const merchantMult = G.namedCharacters?.merchant ? 1.05 : 1;
const received = Math.round(amount * p.rate * merchantMult);
```

**Per-trade delta:**
- Albion (rate 2, 20 wood → gold): 40 → 42 = +2 gold
- Orisk (rate 0.3, 20 food → iron): 6 → 6 = +0 (rounds)
- Thalos (rate 1.5, 20 stone → gold): 30 → 32 = +2 gold
- Meridian (rate 3, 20 gold → food): 60 → 63 = +3 food

**Cumulative scenario estimate:**
- Typical realm: ~30 trades over 200 days (estimated from
  082's fastForward data)
- Average gain per trade: ~1.8 resources (rounded)
- **Cumulative: ~54 extra resources over 200 days**

Not game-breaking. Merchant is a quiet ongoing benefit.

## combat bonus math

`combat.js:updateTowers`:
```js
const smithMult = G.namedCharacters?.smith ? 1.05 : 1;
G.projectiles.push({ ..., damage: 10 * smithMult, ... });
```

**Per-projectile:** 10 → 10.5 damage = +0.5

Raider HPs vary (need to check but let's estimate 20-50
HP). Time to kill a 30-HP raider: 3 hits vs ~2.86 hits =
saves 0.14 hits per raider. At G.speed=1 and 1s fire
cooldown: **~0.14 seconds saved per raider**.

**Per-raid estimate:** 5 raiders × 0.14s = ~0.7s.
**Per-realm estimate:** 40 raids × 0.7s = ~28 seconds.

Smith is the MOST marginal of the 3 mechanics in
practice. +5% damage at discrete integer HP often rounds
to no-extra-hits.

## cumulative verdict

**All 3 bonuses combined impact on a ~200-day realm:**
- Research: +47s
- Trade: +54 resources (not time-sensitive)
- Combat: +28s

**Time saved: ~75 seconds across 5-minute realm = ~25%
speed-up.** Actually that's significant if additive.

BUT these effects are INDEPENDENT — research happens
mostly early-game; combat happens mid-late; trade is
sporadic. So the 75s is spread across the playthrough;
no moment where all 3 stack on the same outcome.

**Conclusion:** mechanics are individually mild and
collectively FORGIVING but not game-breaking. A player
WITHOUT named characters can still complete any scenario;
WITH them, completion is slightly smoother. This matches
the design intent ("graduate decoration to mechanic
without shifting balance much") stated in 101's journal.

## specific findings

### FINDING 1: smith mechanic has the least impact

Integer HP rounding makes +5% damage invisible often.
Consider either:
- Raising to +10% to match teacher's scale
- Or changing the axis (fire-cadence instead of damage)

**Filed as 120 [code] MEDIUM.**

### FINDING 2: teacher bonus diminishes as schools scale

+10% absolute becomes +4% relative at 3 schools. For
Industrial scenario (heavy research), teacher matters
less than in Peaceful. Consider:
- Make it scalable: `teacherBonus = 0.1 + 0.05 * schools`
  (bigger bonus when player invests more)
- Or leave as-is (the flat bonus philosophy is defensible)

**Filed as 120 [code] LOW** — design choice, not a bug.

### FINDING 3: merchant bonus invisible on small trades

20 food × 0.3 rate × 1.05 = 6.3 → rounds to 6. Player
sees no difference. Merchant only matters on trades ≥
~20 resources into high-rate partners.

**Mitigation:** trades this small are probably tutorial-
level anyway. Not a bug; documentation-only.

### FINDING 4: the 3 mechanics don't compose into a
breakpoint

A player who names ALL THREE characters gets ~25% total
game speedup, distributed across systems. There's no
emergent combo where the 3 multiply (e.g., teacher helps
tech that unlocks better smith weapons — no such chain
exists in tech.js, so smith benefit stays linear).

Intentional from 101/102/105 design, but worth noting
that future mechanics should avoid stacking with these
without deliberate balance consideration.

**Filed as 120 [review]** — document stacking rules
before bard/mayor/rival land.

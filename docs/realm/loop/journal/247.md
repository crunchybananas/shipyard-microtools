## why this shape

- **Mirror of 240 bard + 245 smith.** Same
  shape: named character does something
  off-task / observes interior thing.
  Bard composed; smith walked; teacher
  preserves a child's chalk for an extra
  hour.
- **Different texture per character.** Bard's
  was about hidden art; smith's was about
  unexplained departure; teacher's is about
  small preservation — refusing to erase
  for an hour. Each character interiority
  has a distinct emotional register matching
  their canonical role.
- **The CHILD is foregrounded but unnamed.**
  "Wrote their own name, slowly, three
  times" — a small portrait of effort.
  Then the teacher's response: pause +
  hold the trace. Two small interiorities
  in sequence within one beat — child's
  + teacher's.
- **No META-frame** per 213 finding 2.
  The teacher's pause IS the lift line;
  no commentary on whether the realm
  notices.
- **year2 gate** — school has had time to
  accumulate the kind of small student-
  trace that matters. Year-1 school is
  brand new; the moment lands better
  after a year of usage.
- **tag:character** — third use in the
  recent diversification queue (240 bard
  / 245 smith / 247 teacher) all
  character-tagged for non-misc balance
  per 213.

## why this surprises

The realm has 51 NARRATIVE_BEATS entries
+ ~30 BUILDING_FIRST_BEATS + dreams +
nightmares + echoes + raids + seasonal
+ event prose. Across all of those, NO
beat has used **POV INVERSION** — looking
at the realm FROM A CITIZEN'S WORLDVIEW
rather than ABOUT a citizen.

The lift line "the world ends at the
eastern ridge as far as they are
concerned" inverts the usual realm-as-
observer frame. The realm is not
watching the child; the child IS the
realm, totalizing its boundary as the
edge of existence.

This captures the universal small-town
experience of a child who has only ever
known one place — for them, the small
place IS the world. The realm's
chronicle, which the realm itself has
written, is acknowledging that to its
youngest citizens, the realm has no
"outside" to compare to.

## the surprise — dawn-gated beats now fire too

082 HIGH #2 hypothesis: "fastForward may
systematically miss dawn-gated beats (dreams /
stone / nightmare / echo / offering all 0 in 200-
day run)."

083's run shows `dream: 20`, `stone: 1`,
`echo: 2`. The dawn-gated beats ARE firing.

So what happened in 082? Chronicle hit the 300-
entry cap at day 201. The cap drops oldest
entries. With 197 raid-toast entries pouring in
continuously, the 300 most-recent entries were
mostly recent noise. **Day 14's dream, day 131's
stone, day 24's echo all fired — then got
evicted before 082 could sample them.**

082's hypothesis was wrong. The real mechanism was
cap-eviction by noise, not dawn-gate skipping.

**fastForward is trustworthy for dawn-gated beats.**
The tool works as designed; the data 082 saw was
downstream of a chronicle-pollution bug.

**082 HIGH #2 is closed by 083 HIGH #1's fix.**
Both HIGHs in one 3-line change.

### Surprise 2: STRUCTURAL — first QUESTION-opening

**ALL 60 prior NARRATIVE_BEATS open
with a declarative**:
- "There is a/an X..."
- "There comes a/an X..."
- "The Y..."
- "When..."
- Function-text wrappers around the
  above

**301 opens with a QUESTION**: "Why
is the church bell rung at noon and
not at any other hour?"

The question-answer-reflection rhythm
is corpus-novel:
- Q: why is X?
- A: because it always has been
- Reflection: the first instance was
  forgotten before the second

The lift line "the first ringing was
forgotten before the second was held"
captures origin-loss AT THE VERY
MOMENT of practice-establishment.
The realm couldn't remember the
beginning even when it was happening.

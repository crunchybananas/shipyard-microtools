## findings

### HIGH

**HOUSE-ICON "SUPER SMALL" CLAIM
VALIDATED**.

Empirical comparison via screenshots
at z=2.4 and z=3.0:
- Houses render as small white-cone
  shapes with thatched roofs
- Visually MUCH smaller than townhall /
  church / market / blacksmith / etc.
- The contrast is immediate even at
  default zoom

**Root cause analysis** (two contributors,
multiplicative):

1. **`_SPRITE_SIZES` smaller for house**:
   - house: 42×48
   - townhall: 54×56 (+29% width / +17%
     height)
   - castle: 54×68 (+29% width / +42%
     height)
   - church: 48×68 (+14% / +42%)
   - market: 50×46
   - tavern: 46×54
   - All civic/military > house width.

2. **House SVG art fills less of viewBox**:
   - house.svg art occupies ~46×41 within
     128×128 viewBox = 36% × 32%
   - townhall.svg art occupies ~54×36
     within 128×128 viewBox = 42% × 28%
   - Effective rendered art width:
     - house: 42 × (46/128) ≈ **15px**
     - townhall: 54 × (54/128) ≈ **23px**
   - **Houses render at ~65% the visible
     pixel-width of townhalls** — even
     before zoom factor.

**Fix candidates (filed 349 [code]):**
- (A) Bump house `_SPRITE_SIZES` to 50×56
  (close to townhall scale; preserves
  smaller-than-civic-but-not-tiny).
- (B) Revise house.svg art to fill more
  of the 128×128 viewBox (resize body
  + roof to fill ~80% of viewBox).
- (C) Both — bump _SPRITE_SIZES AND
  revise SVG to maximize visual presence.

Recommendation: **(A) is the smallest-
scope fix**; bump to 50×54 (near-civic).
Player perception of houses would jump
~30% larger immediately. (B) is heavier
work (rewriting SVG geometry).

significant for visual-audit cadence:
**always force dayPhase mid-day** for
visual captures unless explicitly
testing night rendering.

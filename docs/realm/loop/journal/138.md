## why this shape

- **Cross-system compound** — needs 072 founders +
  057 cowpen (firstCowpen flag) + autumn/winter
  season + day ≥ 30. Any realm that reaches year 1's
  autumn with a cowpen built will see this fire.
- **Founder2 as shepherd-singer** — 116 established
  founder2 as "sky-watcher"; 097 as "hearth-door-
  stander." 138 adds a 5th surface. Cast now:
  founder1=7, **founder2=5**, founder3=4. Arc is
  fully balanced — no founder with 2x another
  anymore.
- **"The cattle stop moving, briefly, to listen"** —
  voice matches 071 happiness ("Doors stand open;
  songs carry further than they should"). Quiet
  observation; no interpretation.
- **Cold-season gate** — singing at the cowpen in
  summer doesn't hit the same emotional note. Cold
  evenings + animals + singing = pastoral
  intimacy.
- **Day ≥ 30 gate** — ensures the realm has settled.
  Year-1 autumn starts around day 15; 30+ means
  the realm has had ~2 weeks beyond its first
  autumn reveal.
- **Tag: character (reuse per 075)** — aligns with
  034 character intros and 134 namesake beat.
- **Graceful fallback** — "one of the founders" if
  founder2 is somehow absent (shouldn't happen
  given founders_named flag gating, but defensive).
- **No audio cue yet** — could add `onFire:
  'shepherds-song'` later. Filed.

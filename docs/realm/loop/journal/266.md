## why this surprises

Other ambient-entity-grammar beats
(148/152/166) acknowledge SUSTAINED
presences:
- 148 wanderer: returns periodically,
  "no one knows where they go"
- 152 night-shape: recurs at night,
  "no one calls them anything"
- 166 frog-voices: heard in spring rain,
  "two voices"

266 introduces a TRANSIENT presence — the
star is in the sky for an instant, then
gone. The OTHER 3 beats let entities BE
within the realm's awareness; 266 lets an
entity GO before anyone can confirm it.

The lift line "the star is gone before
anyone can ask if anyone else saw it"
captures a NEW philosophical angle: the
SOCIAL IMPOSSIBILITY of shared witness.
The realm watches — but cannot share what
it watched. No other cluster beat
addresses this. This is the surprise.

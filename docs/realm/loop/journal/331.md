## the angle

**NORMALIZATION-THROUGH-ACCUMULATION**:
the realm's accumulated experience
of a recurring cycle (winter)
TRANSFORMS the cycle from EVENT to
BACKGROUND. The third winter feels
indistinguishable from the second;
winter has shifted from notable-
weather-event to ordinary-condition.

Distinct from prior sustained-state
shapes:
- 211 sustained-peace 50d: count
  threshold (no raid days)
- 228 no-death 100d: count threshold
  (days since last death)
- 230 full-pop 60d: ratio threshold
  (pop/maxpop = 1)
- **331 winter-normalized: TRANSFORMATION
  threshold** (recurring cycle becoming
  background)

Prior 3 are MOMENTARY-CROSSINGS of
counts/ratios; 331 is a CHARACTER-OF-
EXPERIENCE crossing — the threshold
is HOW the realm experiences the
season, not a numeric milestone.

Distinct from related angles:
- 254 AUTOMATICITY (action-without-
  thinking; subject is action, not
  season)
- 246 first_thaw (year2+spring; subject
  is moment of seasonal arrival, not
  routinization)

**Lift "Winter has become weather"**
captures the moment when accumulated
experience erases anticipation. The
realm doesn't stop having winter;
the realm stops experiencing winter
AS WINTER (capital-W). It becomes
just another weather condition.

## different infrastructure

The prior 3 sustained-state shapes
all use `G.lastXDay` trackers + save.js
persistence + earned-state gates. 331
uses a DIFFERENT infrastructure:
- Gate: `year3 && G.season === 'winter'`
- No per-realm tracker needed
- The cycle-count is IMPLICIT in
  year3 = "third year" = "third cycle
  of all seasons"

This is fresh infrastructure choice
within the same sub-type. Future
sustained-state shapes can mix
infrastructure (count-trackers vs
seasonal-cycle vs ratio) per fitting.

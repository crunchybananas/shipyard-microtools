## why silent_morning specifically

Of the 4 building-gated year3 beats that
failed in 310 [play]:

| beat | building | prose still coherent? |
|------|----------|----------------------|
| silent_morning | church | **YES** — practice "always knows" itself |
| sea_bell | church | NO — needs the bell-object present |
| church_step_worn | church | NO — needs the door-step present |
| bakery_door_carving | bakery | NO — needs the door present |

silent_morning's lift line "no one has
ever decided which morning; the realm
always knows" describes a **practice**
the realm OWNS independently of substrate.
A realm that once had a church and lost
it can still keep the silent morning.

The other 3 beats describe SPECIFIC OBJECTS
inside the building (bell / step / carved
door) — when the building is destroyed,
the object is too. Their prose collapses
without current presence. Per 257 anti-
completionist: don't gate-relax when
prose collapses.

This is INFRASTRUCTURE THAT'S READY: future
surprise tick can author fallback-variant
prose for sea_bell / church_step / bakery
("the church is gone but...") and use
everHadBuilding. Or 350 milestone-letter
can document the partial-coverage decision
as design.

- **Mirror of 240 bard_unsung_song.** Same
  shape (named-character does something
  off-task; tag:character; once-per-realm;
  evocative closing). 240 had bard composing
  songs no one heard; 245 has smith walking
  the river in silence.
- **NEW: micro land-as-agent inside character
  beat.** "The fire cools by a degree, and
  waits." gives the FORGE FIRE quiet agency
  WITHIN a character-tagged beat. Land-as-
  agent shape was at 3 uses (207/227/229);
  245 nestles a 4th micro-instance INSIDE a
  different sub-type. Fits within cluster's
  observed-pattern frame; doesn't promote
  land-as-agent.
- **No META-frame** per 213 finding 2. Smith
  has interiority (walking, reasons-not-
  named); the fire has agency (cools and
  waits). No commentary on whether the realm
  knows.
- **Tag: character** — second non-misc tag
  ship since 214 (after 240 bard); continues
  213 diversification queue.
- **Earned-state gate.** smith named via
  ensureSmith hook on blacksmith build (034
  + line 238). day ≥ 30 ensures smith has
  been around ~20+ days. Summer/autumn —
  river-walking weather, fire-cooling
  meaningful before winter shutters
  everything.
- **Closing image "and waits".** Two-word
  punctuation. Same crafting principle as
  227/229 closing lines — the beat ends with
  a small lift beyond the literal moment.

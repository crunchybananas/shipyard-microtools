# 065 — the-fixer (research-completion beats)

**Challenge:** `the-fixer` — accepted. Closes 060's
widest content gap: the research/progression axis
had zero narrative surface. Techs finished with a
sound cue and a "Research complete: X" toast, but
no chronicle entry. A realm that mastered every
tech left no record of having done so.

## the fix

`docs/realm/js/tech.js`:

1. Import `chronicle` from story.js (new import)
2. New `RESEARCH_BEATS` table (11 entries for the
   researchable techs; agriculture/forestry are
   free at start so they don't fire)
3. In `updateResearch` at completion, call
   `chronicle(text, 'research')` with lookup +
   fallback

`docs/realm/js/story.js`:

4. Add `'research'` to `_ECHO_SOURCE_TAGS` so the
   new beats can be echoed by 059's memory system.
   Bundled here because introducing a new tag AND
   making it echo-eligible is one thematic change.

## the voice

Tone-matched to `BUILDING_FIRST_BEATS`: one sentence,
imagistic, no NPC names (that's 034's space), no
dialog quotes (character space), no exclamation.
Each beat frames the tech as a shift in how the
realm knows itself:

```
masonry:      'The realm masters masonry. Stone now speaks in lines and courses.'
engineering:  'Engineering is grasped. Water runs where it is told; the roads remember where they lead.'
metallurgy:   'Ore yields to fire at last. The realm learns to fetch the buried heat.'
commerce:     'Commerce is taught. Coins begin to remember which hands they have passed through.'
military:     'The art of arms is set down in lore. Recruits study what they once only endured.'
brewing:      'Brewing is learned. What cold spring water could not cheer, malted barley will.'
architecture: 'Architecture is learned. Thresholds now know where walls begin and where they end.'
baking:       'Baking is mastered. Bread cools on a shelf the village knows the shape of.'
husbandry:    'Husbandry is grasped. Hoof and horn are counted, named, remembered.'
smithing:     'Smithing is learned. Hammers now strike with purpose beyond force.'
archery:      "The fletcher's trade is mastered. Arrows fly true to measure, not to hope."
```

Fallback string for any future tech without a
specific entry: `"${tech.name} is mastered. The
realm bends a new skill into its lore."` — so new
techs never fall through silently.

## why this shape

Considered:

- **A single generic beat** ("research complete")
  — too flat. The whole point of giving research
  a narrative surface is that each tech is a
  distinct moment of change.
- **Beats driven by buildings unlocked** — masonry
  unlocks quarry/wall/church, so the masonry beat
  could reference those. Rejected: would confuse
  the axis (building-beat vs tech-beat) and some
  techs unlock multiple buildings.
- **Dynamic text built from `tech.unlocks`** —
  same issue + makes future refactoring harder.

Static per-tech table is the cleanest shape. Same
pattern as BUILDING_FIRST_BEATS; same maintenance
story.

## what this adds to the narrative stack

The stack is now 8 layers:

1. First-build beats (`milestone`)
2. Named characters (`character`)
3. Population thresholds (`milestone`)
4. Castle (`victory`) + Raid (`raid`)
5. Dreams (`dream`)
6. Nightmare (`nightmare`) — once per realm
7. Stone (`stone`) — once per realm
8. Echoes (`echo`)
9. **Research (`research`)** — NEW
10. Season transitions (main.js, `season`)
11. Events (events.js, `event`)
12. Deaths / Births (combat+economy, `death`/`birth`)
13. Approach boosts (no tag — alters dream content)

For a realm that completes all 11 techs, that's
11 additional chronicle entries spread across
game-days (research takes 250-500 ticks each;
spreads out naturally). 060's audit expected
"silent stretches of 9 days between dream fires"
— 065 fills those stretches on progression-active
realms.

And with `research` in `_ECHO_SOURCE_TAGS`, future
echoes can cite them: *"The bards still sing of
that day: 'Smithing is learned. Hammers now
strike with purpose beyond force.'"*

## what I did NOT do

- **Add a tech-tree-complete milestone beat**
  ("The realm has mastered every art known to
  its people."). Tempting; filed below. Would
  fire once if all 11 are researched.
- **Fire on the `agriculture` or `forestry`**
  free-unlocks. Those don't go through the
  research timer; they're default-known. Adding
  day-1 beats for them would double up with the
  "A new realm is founded…" founding beat.
- **Tie research beats to the approach system**
  (064). Research isn't a once-per-realm event,
  so the approach mechanism doesn't apply.
- **Visual/audio effects.** The existing
  `playSound('mission')` + `showToast(...)`
  already handle the "moment of completion"
  feedback. Chronicle is for narrative memory.
- **Touch BUILDING_FIRST_BEATS**. Research and
  building beats are sibling but not unified.
  The future NARRATIVE_BEATS idea (062 filed)
  could merge them; out of scope for 065.

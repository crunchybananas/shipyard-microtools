## why this shape

- **Non-founder.** Focal is WEATHER and MEMORY — a
  thing that happened before current citizens were
  born. No `G.storyFlags.founder*` references.
- **Intergenerational without names.** "The children
  born after do not believe it" implies time passing
  and multiple generations without relying on
  founder-arc bookkeeping. Founders may or may not
  be the ones remembering; the realm's COLLECTIVE
  memory is the agent.
- **Year-2 gate.** Fires in autumn OF year 2 or
  later. A realm that lives 1 year doesn't see it.
  Matches the "remembering something the youngest
  don't" shape — requires time to have passed.
- **Tag: `event` (reuse per 075 invariant).** Same
  tag as 142 distant-letter. "A thing happened to
  the realm from outside/beyond ordinary life."
  Echo-eligible via 063.
- **5 variants via kingdom-hash.** Deterministic;
  each kingdom has ITS storm. Cross-reload
  consistent.
- **Uses kingdom name in one variant.** "${kname}"
  lives inside the first storm's template string —
  interpolation happens when the text composes,
  not at variant-pick time. Preserves uniqueness.
- **No characters named** at all. Even 034 named-
  characters (mayor, smith, etc) not referenced.
  Reader imagines who's remembering.

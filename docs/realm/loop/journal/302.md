**MEDIUM finding (real bug)**:
`G.stats.scenariosWon` was MISSING from
newGame's stats reset (line 324). State.js
initial G.stats includes
`scenariosWon: []` (state.js:154), but
newGame's stats-reset object OMITS it.

**Risk**: main.js:531 calls
`G.stats.scenariosWon.includes(G.scenario)`
inside the scenario-victory dispatch. If
a player restarts mid-scenario and then
COMPLETES a scenario, the `.includes()`
call would TypeError on undefined.

This is the kind of latent bug that 271
[code] audit was filed to find. 31 ticks
of deferral revealed it.

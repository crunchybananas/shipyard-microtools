## why this shape

- **NEW SUB-TYPE — not sub-type-completion.**
  Per 203 positive authoring rule. Habituation-
  recognition is genuinely distinct from the
  existing 8 sub-types: it's the realm
  observing its OWN accumulated experience as
  the trigger, not an external condition (peace
  / no-death / full-pop) or land-agency
  (land/water/hearth) or weather endpoints
  (frost/thaw). Second-order observation.
- **The lift line: "the fire is set without
  thinking now."** Automaticity is the proof
  of habituation. The realm's behavior is the
  evidence. Concrete-mundane → meta-claim with
  no hand-waving.
- **"Some are remembered separately and the
  rest blur into one long evening."** Captures
  the textured-memory phenomenology — not all
  nights equal. Storms / raids / festivals /
  deaths get remembered; routine nights merge.
  Models how human memory of repetition
  actually works.
- **Year-2 + autumn/winter gate.** Year-2
  ensures enough night accumulation for the
  prose to land (year-1 is too few). Autumn/
  winter is when the night-falls-differently
  observation lands — summer/spring nights
  are short, less weighty.
- **Universal static prose.** Every realm
  experiences this regardless of kingdom name
  or specific events. No founder-hash, no
  variant pool. Single prose.
- **Misc tag.** Joins the broad observational
  cluster. No new tag warranted.

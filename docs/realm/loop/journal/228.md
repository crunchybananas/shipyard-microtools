## why this shape

- **Mirror of 211** — same 3-piece pattern
  (tracker + persistence + beat). Establishes
  sustained-state-recognition as a reusable
  shape, not a one-off.
- **100-day threshold (vs 211's 50-day).**
  Death is rarer baseline than raids — needs
  a longer absence to read as remarkable.
  Citizens die from starvation, raids,
  combat. A realm that's avoided ALL of
  these for 100 days is genuinely fortunate.
- **earned-state gate `citizensDied >= 1`** —
  ensures the realm has FACED death.
  Otherwise the beat fires for any new
  fortunate realm, which is meaningless.
- **Avoids META-frame** per 213 finding 2.
  The beat describes the GRAVE FIELD as
  having weight; doesn't comment on whether
  the realm-as-collective notices.
- **Sub-type 1 → 2 uses** promotes toward
  the 3+ threshold. One more
  sustained-state shipment (filed candidates:
  peak happiness 30 days; full population 60
  days; no failed harvest 2 years) and the
  pattern becomes invariant.
- **Doc updates SAME tick** per 204+208
  process discipline.

## why this shape

- **New narrative surface** — once per realm, a quiet
  "deep winter night" beat. Different from first-snow
  (088, which fires at any dawn of first winter). This
  one waits for DEEP NIGHT of winter, not dawn.
- **Trigger composition**: winter + day ≥ 22 + dayPhase
  > 0.85 × dayLength = late-night phase. `G.dayLength
  || 3600` fallback keeps it safe during initialization.
- **Day 22 gate** = first winter's day 1 (years are 28-
  day, seasons 7-day; winter is days 22-28 of year 1;
  50-56 of year 2; etc). After 22, ANY winter's late-
  night qualifies, but the flag prevents re-fire so
  only the FIRST deep-winter-night ever sees it.
- **founder3 as "night-watcher"** — 4th canonical role
  for founder3 after (089 nightmare pool, 097 dream
  harvest thread, 115 audio). Mirrors founder1's
  winter-count-elder arc (093) but in a nocturnal
  register.
- **Graceful fallback** — unnamed-founder realms get
  "The watchers at the fires do not speak." (plural,
  collective). Maintains voice.
- **Tag: milestone (reuse per 075).** "The realm
  noticed a thing for the first time" is the same
  category as first-snow/constellation.
- **No audio cue** — kept scope tight. A future
  composer tick could add `onFire: 'longest-night'`
  with a low hum or wolf-howl-distant (ambient
  winter-night texture).

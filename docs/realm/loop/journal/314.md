## triple-axis ship

to surprise on ALL THREE generative

**REGISTER axis — 5th OUTSIDE register:**
- META 263 (paradoxical-self-reference)
- JOYFUL 296 (collective-ease)
- WONDER 297 (curious-without-resolving)
- IRRITATION 303 (negative-domesticated)
- **TERROR 314** (acute-fear /
  dread-without-object)

TERROR is the second NEGATIVE-tone
register (after IRRITATION) but
qualitatively different: IRRITATION
is cultural-of-imperfection and
RESOLVES through ritual; TERROR is
acute, ungrounded, and DOES NOT
resolve. The realm experiences
collective freezing without being
able to name what triggered it.

**ANGLE axis — DREAD-WITHOUT-CAUSE:**

Distinct from prior 4 OUTSIDE angles
because:
- META = self-reference paradox
- JOYFUL = positive emotion ease
- WONDER = curious mystery without
  resolving (acknowledges; chooses to
  remain)
- IRRITATION = familiar annoyance
  domesticated (reverses to value)
- TERROR + DREAD = unfounded foreboding
  the realm cannot resolve

The non-event ("no bell rang") is the
unsettling thing. The realm's expectation
of a bell creates the dread when the
bell doesn't arrive.

**STRUCTURE axis — 5th non-declarative
opening: NEGATION:**
- 301 noon_bell — QUESTION ("Why...")

  ("Listen for...")

- **314 morning_dread — NEGATION

NEGATION makes the reader notice what
ISN'T. Distinct from prior 4 STRUCTURAL
forms (question/imperative/second-person/
dialog) which all make the reader notice
what IS. Foregrounding absence is a
different cognitive operation.

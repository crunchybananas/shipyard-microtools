## why this surprises

This is the **FIRST JOYFUL-REGISTER
beat in the corpus**. Across 58 prior
NARRATIVE_BEATS entries, the
emotional register has been
- contemplative (observational-elder)
- somber (forgetting / requiem)
- meditative (character-interiority)
- META-paradoxical (263)
- philosophical (transient-witness /
  inheritance / etc.)

NONE has been **DYNAMIC + JOYFUL**.
296 introduces the realm LAUGHING —
not observing or reflecting, but
RELEASING into ease.

The lift line "doesn't have to pay
attention to itself" captures EASE
as the OPPOSITE of vigilance. The
realm relaxed enough to laugh
without worrying about why. Self-
forgetfulness as wellness.

This is genuinely new emotional
ground for the corpus.

angles continue to surprise.

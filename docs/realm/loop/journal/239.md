## why this shape

- **20% bump matches 238 critic's
  recommendation.** Per-building inspection
  preserves silhouette character — towers
  grow taller than wider; squat structures
  (market/bakery) grow more uniformly.
- **No structural change.** `drawSpriteIfReady`
  reads from the table; only the values
  change. Phase B step 2 envelope (1.1× scale,
  +3y) still applies on top.
- **Bigger sprites still anchor at bottom-
  center on (s.x, s.y).** Buildings sit
  correctly on tile centers; just look ~20%
  larger.
- **Comment captures the empirical-data
  story.** "Original sized at zoom 1.0;
  empirical audit at zoom 1.3 (default)
  showed undersizing." Future readers see why
  the bump landed.

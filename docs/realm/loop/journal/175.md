## what tavern.svg expresses that canvas struggles with

- **Chain links between bracket and sign board.**
  Canvas attaches the sign board solidly to the
  bracket; SVG ships 2 small ellipse links
  suggesting the board swings from the bracket
  on chains. Reads as "wind-stirred."
- **Cobblestone path leading to door.** Canvas
  tavern has no path; SVG adds 5 ovals of
  varying tones forming a short path — implies
  this tavern gets enough foot traffic to wear
  the ground.
- **Door slightly-ajar warm crack of light.**
  Canvas door is a solid dark rect; SVG adds a
  thin orange line along the door's left edge
  reading as "light leaking from inside —
  someone's home." Combined with the larger
  hearth glow at the door area, makes the
  tavern feel actively occupied.
- **Hanging harvest bundle.** Canvas tavern has
  no exterior decoration beyond the sign
  board; SVG adds a small sheaf-shape bundle
  hanging by the door — agricultural prosperity
  signal.
- **Half-timber posts on wall.** Canvas has flat
  rect walls with horizontal plank lines; SVG
  adds 3 vertical timber posts at ~45% alpha
  giving Tudor character (matching house.svg's
  treatment).
- **Roof-top flag with sunlit band.** Canvas
  flag is a flat rect at single color; SVG ships
  the gold board + bright accent band + outline
  for edge definition.

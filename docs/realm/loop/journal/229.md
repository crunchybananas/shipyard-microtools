  the SHAPE of agency, not repeat. 207 was
  broad-collective; 227 was specific-object;
  229 is RELATIONAL keeper (the hearth holds

  in itself).

  hearth needs names to hold. Year-3 means
  the realm has lived long enough to have
  history; citizensDied ≥ 1 means at least
  one name to memorialize.

  either way: hoist to its own observed-

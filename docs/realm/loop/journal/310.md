### MEDIUM — building-gated year3 beats never fired in raid-heavy play

4 of 12 expected beats failed to fire
despite year3 transitioning at d57:

```
✓ noon_bell_origin (year2 + church)        d29
✗ silent_morning (year3 + church)
✓ path_knows_routine (year3)               d57
✓ wagon_track (year3)                      d57
✓ unplaceable_sound (year2)                d29
✓ realm_laughs (year2 + happiness>65)      d29
✗ church_step_worn (year3 + church)
✗ bakery_door_carving (year3 + bakery)
✗ sea_bell (year3 + church)
✓ phrase_misheard (year3)                  d57
✓ liminal_moment (year2 + dayPhase>0.6)    d29
✓ nights_blur (year2 + autumn/winter)      d15
```

ALL 4 missed beats are year3 + church OR
year3 + bakery. The 4 year3-only beats
all fired (path_knows_routine,
wagon_track, phrase_misheard) — the
gate is the building check.

**At end of run: G.buildings.length = 0.**
First raid at d59 (~ 2 days after year3);
multiple subsequent raids destroyed all
structures by d~120.

**The narrative-coverage problem:** in
raid-heavy gameplay, year3 gates check at
d57 but the buildings may already be lost
to attrition. In this run, between d29
(noon_bell fired with church present) and
d57 (year3 transition), at least one raid
destroyed the church before silent_morning
could fire.

**Design intent:** beats need their building
present AT the gate moment. silent_morning
saying "the church keeps a silent morning"
without a church is incoherent.

**Empirical impact:** in 240 simulated
days, raid-heavy realms saw 4/12 beats =
**33% narrative-coverage degradation**.
The realm experiences MOST of its lore in
peaceful gameplay; raid-heavy gameplay sees
substantially less.

**Severity MEDIUM.** This is by design
(beats require their substrate) but the
empirical degradation in default scenario
is worth noting. A 350 milestone-letter
hopes/fears could explicitly acknowledge:
*"narrative coverage in raid-heavy
gameplay is partial and that is OK."*

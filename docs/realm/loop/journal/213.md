## concern 2 (MEDIUM): 212 prose may be too meta

**The line:** "No one sees. The realm goes
on without seeing."

**The problem:** this calls out the chronicle's
omniscience in a way that breaks the implicit
fiction. The chronicle has always been a
kind of OMNISCIENT narrator — recording what
happens whether or not characters witness it.
By saying "no one sees" then implying the
chronicle DID see (because we're reading
about it), the prose creates a mild paradox.

It's clever. It might be TOO clever.

**Counter-argument:** the line acknowledges
something true about the realm — that not
every moment is collectively known. The
META-frame is the surprising angle 203
positive rule rewards.

**Skeptic's counter-counter:** other beats
in the cluster have surprising angles
WITHOUT breaking fiction. 207 fields_know
gave the LAND agency (fresh axis, fictionally
coherent). 211 sustained_peace gave PEACE a
weight (fresh axis, fictionally coherent).
212 reaches OUT of the fiction to comment
on the chronicle as a witness.

That's a different KIND of move. It might be
the right move once. It would be the wrong
move three times.

**Recommendation:** if individual-interiority
sub-type accumulates 2 more uses (per 212
filing), the META-frame should NOT repeat.
Future individual-interiority beats should
keep the citizen-focal angle WITHOUT the
"the realm doesn't see" reflexivity. Otherwise
the cluster develops a tic.
